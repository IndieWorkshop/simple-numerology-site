FAQ

Does the app work offline?

Yes. All numerology data and calculations are fully offline. Inputs and results stay on your device.

Why do I see ads?

Ads keep the app free. We use Google AdMob. If tracking is disabled on iOS, we request non‑personalized ads.

How do I change notification settings?

Open your iPhone settings → Notifications → Numerology to enable or disable alerts.

How do I update or correct my name/birth date?

Go to the Profile/Inputs screen inside the app and update your details. Your readings will refresh automatically.

How do refunds work?

All purchases and subscriptions (if available in the future) are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com.

How can I request data deletion?

All data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the app removes stored data. For questions, contact us via email.

Troubleshooting

  • No daily reading? Check your device date/time and notification permissions.
  • Wrong language? The app supports English, Ukrainian, and Russian. Set your preferred language in iOS Settings → General → Language & Region.
  • App crashes or freezes? Restart the app or reboot your device. If the issue persists, email us with steps to reproduce.
