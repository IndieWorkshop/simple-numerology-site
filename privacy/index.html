Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

1. Data Collection

  • Offline numerology data: The app performs numerology calculations fully offline. Your inputs (such as names, dates of birth, and results) are stored only on your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Ads and analytics: We use Google AdMob to show ads and Firebase Analytics to understand app usage. These services may collect device and interaction data according to their own policies.

2. iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT)

On iOS, the app may ask for permission to track your activity across apps and websites (ATT). If you decline, we request non-personalized ads. You can change your tracking permission at any time in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking, and you may reset your device’s advertising identifier (IDFA) in iOS settings.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Limited information is shared with Google AdMob and Firebase to provide ads and analytics. For details, see: Google AdMob and Firebase.

4. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

5. Your Choices

  • iOS (ATT): Manage tracking permission in Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking. If tracking is disabled, ads will be non-personalized.
  • Ad personalization: You can also manage Google ad personalization via your Google account ad settings or your device’s advertising settings.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with a new effective date.

7. Contact

If you have any questions, please contact us at support email.
